# Business Requirements Document (BRD)
## Webinar Registration Website

**Version:** 1.0  
**Requestor:** Marketing Department, CM Corp  
**Purpose:** Capture the business requirements as stated by the Marketing Department for a webinar registration website

---

## 1. Business Demand

**The Marketing Department** requests the creation of a simple, user-friendly **website** to promote an upcoming webinar.

The site must include information about the event and a **signup form** for invitees to register their participation.

---

## 2. Business Requirements

### 2.1 Webinar Information Display

A dedicated webpage outlining the webinar topic, date, time, agenda, speaker profiles, and any other details.

### 2.2 Signup Form Integration

A functional registration form to collect attendee information such as name, email address, company, and job title. The form should have data validation to ensure accuracy and completeness.

### 2.3 Scalability

The infrastructure should handle expected traffic spikes, especially as the webinar date approaches.

### 2.4 Timely Deployment

The website must be live before the marketing campaign launch to align with the marketing campaign schedule.

---

## 3. Stakeholders

**Primary Stakeholder:** Marketing Department  
**Secondary Stakeholders:** 
- IT Operations (infrastructure support)
- IT Security (compliance and security review)
- Executive Sponsor (budget approval)
